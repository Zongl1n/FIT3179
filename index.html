Australian FLIGHTS

Australian Cities Map

This map displays the main cities in Australia along with the number of departing flights. Larger circles represent higher volumes of traffic, helping to identify the busiest air travel hubs in the country. Use the airline selection to view data specific to different carriers.

On-time Performance

This gauge displays the percentage of flights that departed or arrived on time. A higher percentage indicates better punctuality, which is key for passenger satisfaction and operational efficiency of airlines and airports.

Cancellation Rate

This gauge shows the percentage of flights that were canceled. A lower rate reflects better reliability, making it a critical metric for understanding operational challenges and passenger impact.

Australian States Flight Volume Map

This map illustrates the distribution of flight volumes across different states in Australia. Darker shades indicate areas with more flight activity, helping to visualize regional air traffic intensity.

On-time Performance Comparison

Compare on-time performance between arrivals and departures with this chart. It reveals any inconsistencies in flight punctuality, offering insights into potential areas for improvement.

Monthly Flight Counts per Year

This heatmap shows the number of flights each month over multiple years. Darker shades indicate busier months, making it easier to spot seasonal trends in air travel.

Flight Arrivals vs Departures

This pie chart visualizes the proportion of arrivals versus departures for each city. It helps identify cities that serve as major transit hubs or destinations.

Overall Flight On-time Performance

This line chart tracks the trend of on-time performance over the years, allowing a deeper understanding of long-term improvements or challenges in flight punctuality.

Introduction

Welcome to the Australian Flights Dashboard. This dashboard provides insights into the air travel landscape of Australia, including city-specific traffic, airline punctuality, and cancellation rates. Use the interactive elements to explore different aspects of air travel data and understand the dynamics of Australia’s aviation sector.
